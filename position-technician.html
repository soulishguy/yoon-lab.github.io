Lab Technician

Now Recruiting

RPBS Lab · Dept. of Intelligent Systems Engineering · Indiana University Bloomington

About the Role

The Remote Programmable Biosystems (RPBS) Lab is seeking a highly motivated Lab Technician to support the launch and daily operations of our new laboratory. This is a unique opportunity to be part of the founding team of a cutting-edge lab at the intersection of ultrasound engineering and synthetic biology.

The Lab Technician will play a central role in maintaining lab infrastructure, supporting experiments, managing inventory and safety protocols, and ensuring the smooth operation of research activities.

Key Responsibilities

  • Set up, maintain, and calibrate laboratory equipment including ultrasound systems, cell culture facilities, and imaging instruments
  • Prepare reagents, media, buffers, and other materials required for experiments
  • Assist researchers with routine and specialized experimental procedures (cell culture, molecular cloning, protein assays, etc.)
  • Manage lab inventory, ordering supplies, and coordinating with vendors
  • Maintain compliance with IU Environmental Health & Safety (EHS) protocols, including chemical hygiene and biosafety
  • Organize and maintain lab records, equipment logs, and standard operating procedures (SOPs)
  • Support onboarding and training of new lab members

Qualifications

  • Required: B.S. in biology, biomedical engineering, biochemistry, or a related field
  • Required: At least 1 year of hands-on laboratory experience (academic or industry)
  • Experience with mammalian cell culture and sterile technique
  • Familiarity with standard molecular biology techniques (PCR, gel electrophoresis, cloning)
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a team environment

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with flow cytometry, fluorescence microscopy, or bioimaging
  • Familiarity with ultrasound equipment or acoustic systems
  • Experience managing lab safety protocols or EHS compliance
  • Basic proficiency in data analysis tools (Python, MATLAB, or R)

What We Offer

  • A collaborative, supportive, and innovative research environment
  • Opportunity to contribute to high-impact biomedical research from the ground up
  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities within the Luddy School at IU Bloomington
  • Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits through Indiana University

How to Apply

Please send a single PDF containing your CV and a brief cover letter describing your relevant experience and interest in the position to cyoon@iu.edu with the subject line "Lab Technician Application — [Your Name]".

Review of applications will begin immediately and continue until the position is filled.
